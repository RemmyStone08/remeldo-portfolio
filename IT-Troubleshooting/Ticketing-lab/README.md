# IT Help Desk Ticketing Simulation

---

## Project Overview

This project simulates a real-world IT help desk environment based on hands-on experience managing repair tickets in a production system.

It demonstrates the ability to:

- Log and manage tickets
- Troubleshoot hardware/software issues
- Document resolutions professionally
- Follow a structured ticket lifecycle

---

## Environment

- Ticketing System: Simulated (based on real POS/ticket workflow)
- Role: IT Support Technician
- Devices: Laptops, desktops, peripherals

---

## Ticket Lifecycle

1. Ticket Created
2. Ticket Assigned
3. Troubleshooting and Diagnosis
4. Resolution applied.
5. Ticket Clo9sed with Notes.

---

## Sample Tickets 

---

###🧾 Ticket #001 – Laptop Not Powering On

Status: Closed
Priority: High

Issue:
Customer reported that the laptop does not power on.

Troubleshooting Steps:

- Tested charger with known working device
- Removed battery and attempted direct power
- Inspected DC jack for damage

Findings:
No power response from motherboard.

Resolution:
Diagnosed motherboard failure. Recommended replacement.

---

###🌐 Ticket #002 – WiFi Not Detecting Networks

Status: Resolved
Priority: Medium

Issue:
Laptop unable to detect available WiFi networks.

Troubleshooting Steps:

- Checked Device Manager for adapter status
- Reinstalled WiFi drivers
- Reset network settings

Findings:
WiFi adapter disabled via BIOS setting.

Resolution:
Enabled wireless adapter in BIOS. Connectivity restored.

---

###🖥️ Ticket #003 – System Running Extremely Slow

Status: Closed
Priority: Medium

Issue:
User reported slow performance during normal usage.

Troubleshooting Steps:

- Checked startup programs
- Scanned for malware
- Reviewed disk usage

Findings:
High disk usage caused by background applications.

Resolution:
Disabled unnecessary startup apps and optimized system.

--- 

###🔊 Ticket #004 – No Sound Output

Status: Closed
Priority: Low

Issue:
No audio output from speakers or headphones.

Troubleshooting Steps:

- Checked audio drivers
- Verified playback devices
- Tested with external headphones

Findings:
Corrupt audio driver.

Resolution:
Reinstalled correct audio driver. Audio restored.

---

###🔌 Ticket #005 – Laptop Not Charging

Status: Closed
Priority: High

Issue:
Device plugged in but not charging.

Troubleshooting Steps:

- Tested with another charger
- Inspected charging port
- Checked battery health

Findings:
Faulty charging port.

Resolution:
Replaced DC charging port. Device charging normally.
